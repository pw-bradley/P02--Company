Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Celestial Sweets is a small buisness themed cafe which sells a mixture of tea, coffee and pastries. They can be either ordered for events or a table can be reserved for some inside eating.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

The company wants to provide customers with a delicious foods and calm eating environment to bring your friends to.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

The company aims to entice customers with its themed ambiance of space-based foods as well as a calm environment to meet with friends.

Customer Personas

Persona 1

Fiona Wu is a single 27 year old library technician looking for a girlfriend. She's been talking to what she believes is a potential match and would like to meet her in person. A year prior she had a unhealthy relationship with her previous partner and wants to go to safe location where she will have the opportunity to talk to her potential match

Persona 2

Eric Davis is a 44 year old Regional Director and a single father of 3. He has a son who graduating from high school very soon. Has no baking experience and does not have the time to learn. However he wants to bring home something nice to celebrate his son's hard work that they can all enjoy as a family during a board game night.

Persona 3

Lake Williams is a 19 year old English-Major university student with a growing Instagram following. They've gained followers over the years by taking pictures of both their dog and places that they have visited with their friends. They want to go somewhere which will allow for oppertunities for unique photographs and nice environment to be with their friends.
